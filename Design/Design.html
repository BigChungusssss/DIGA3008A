Possible designs

These are designs for each page of my website. The blog cards are designed to display a brief summary of each blog post, giving visitors a quick overview before diving in. The profile section will be integrated into the homepage to maintain a clean and unified layout. Both the blog and essay pages will follow a similar structure, styled in an article-like format that combines the written content with its corresponding design section for consistency and readability.

Font

Font gelasio:The font I’ve chosen for this project is Gelasio. I selected it because it closely resembles the Georgia typeface, which is prominently used in the user interface of Bloodborne. My website is inspired by the aesthetic of Bloodborne’s Hunter’s Dream — a quiet, mysterious refuge for Hunters. The Gelasio font is both elegant and readable, and provides the the website with a otherworldly feel which the hunters dream provides.

Colour Palette

My color palette will primarily consist of black, white, and various shades of blue. These colors were carefully chosen to reflect the visual atmosphere of Bloodborne’s Hunter’s Dream — a space dominated by cool, desaturated tones. The use of blue, in particular, evokes a sense of calm, melancholy, and mystery, while black and white provide contrast and clarity. Together, this palette creates a dreamlike, otherworldly mood thats found in the Hunters dream.
