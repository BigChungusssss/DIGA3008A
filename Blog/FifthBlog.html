Steam

Steam is the digital distribution platform owned by the Valve Corporation.

Pros:

  • It has easy navigation
  • Very responsive design
  • Game pages are very informative

Cons:

  • The UI is very outdated and cluttered
  • Inconsistent visual hierarchy
  • Overuse of pop-ups

Nexus Mods

Nexus Mods is a popular online website, where people can download and share mods for video games.

Pros:

  • It has a clear and organized layout
  • Has game specific pages, which allows users to find mods easier
  • Mod pages are well detailed

Cons:

  • Intrusive ads
  • Cluttered homepage
